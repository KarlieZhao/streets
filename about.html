Karlie Zhao | 趙文然

I'm currently an undergraduate student at School of Creative Media, City University of Hong Kong. At SCM, I'm very fortunate to have experiences in multiple aspects of art, with people who came from diverse background. My interests cover a range of interdisciplinary areas, including painting, film and film study, documentary, animation, generative art and programming.

With the social movement that happened in 2019 in Hong Kong, I found myself very interested in observing and documenting the ever-changing and controversial social environment that we are living in. I hope that through attending this shared campus programme, I can get closer to the social study area, sharing ideas about ownership, identity and belonging in public places, and explore ways to engage these social issues in my art practice.

Having a background in film production, I have experiences and skills in high-quality video shooting, location sound recording and post-editing. I gained the fundamental knowledge of documentary and built critical thinking through reflecting on the issues raised in the documentaries and hands-on practices. Hopefully, these experiences will be helpful in our future workshops.
